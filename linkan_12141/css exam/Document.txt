Project Title: FreshCart – Online Grocery Store (CSS3 Task)
Project Description:
This project is a visually appealing and user-centric eCommerce website template designed for an online grocery delivery business. Built using HTML5 and CSS3, the layout is clean, responsive, and optimized for user experience. The design simulates a real-world platform for selling groceries, daily essentials, and personal care products, with a focus on accessibility, ease of navigation, and promotional visibility.



Key Features:
1.Top Navigation Bar:
Language selection, coupons, and promotional banner.
Search bar with product and location filters.
Quick access to user profile, cart, and wishlist.
1.Header & Menu:
Prominent logo: FreshCart
Full navigation menu: Shop, Stores, Mega Menu, Pages, Account, Dashboard, Docs
Drop-down menus for blog, account pages, and error pages.
1.Hero Banner:
Promotional highlight: Free Shipping on orders over $100
Visually rich with fresh food imagery and clear call-to-action.
1.Featured Categories:
Quick-access category icons for: Baby Care, Chicken/Meat/Fish, Cleaning Essentials, Pet Care, Fruits & Vegetables, etc.
1.Category Promotions:
Highlight cards for deals like 30% off on Fruits & Vegetables and 25% off on Freshly Baked Buns
1.Popular Products Section:
Display of trending grocery items with:
Product image, title, pricing (with discounts), star ratings, and “Add to Cart” buttons.
Deals like Buy 1 Get 1, 40% Off, Hot Sale
1.Service Highlights:
Four benefit cards:
10 Minute Grocery Delivery
Best Prices & Offers
Wide Assortment
Easy Returns
1.Footer Section:
Organized links to all categories, consumer help, company information, and shopper opportunities.
App store links and social media icons.
Payment partner logos and copyright.
Technologies Used:
HTML5 for semantic layout
CSS3 for responsive styling and layout structuring
